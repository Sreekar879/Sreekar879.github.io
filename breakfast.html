Delicious Breakfast Recipes

Simple Omelette

  • Beat 2 eggs, salt, and pepper; pour into a skillet.
  • Cook gently, add fillings, fold, and serve.

Fluffy Pancakes

  • Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • Combine milk, egg, butter, and vanilla; mix with dry ingredients.
  • Cook on a griddle until golden; serve with toppings.

Homemade Granola

  • Combine oats, nuts, seeds, spices, honey, and oil.
  • Bake until golden, cool, and mix with dried fruits.
