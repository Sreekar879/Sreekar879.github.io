Quick & Easy Lunch Recipes

Classic Caesar Salad

  • Toss romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing, croutons, and Parmesan.
  • Add grilled chicken or shrimp for extra protein.

Avocado & Turkey Sandwich

  • Layer turkey, avocado, lettuce, and tomato on whole-grain bread.
  • Add mustard or mayo and a slice of cheese.

Tomato Basil Soup

  • Simmer crushed tomatoes, basil, garlic, and onion.
  • Blend until smooth and serve with a dollop of cream.
