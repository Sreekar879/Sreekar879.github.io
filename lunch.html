Quick & Easy Lunch Recipes

Classic Caesar Salad

A timeless favorite that's easy to whip up for a refreshing lunch.

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Grilled chicken or shrimp (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toss chopped romaine lettuce with Caesar dressing, croutons, and shredded Parmesan cheese.
  2. Add grilled chicken or shrimp for a protein boost.
  3. Serve chilled.

Avocado & Turkey Sandwich

A delicious and healthy sandwich that packs a flavorful punch.

Ingredients:

  • Whole-grain bread
  • Sliced turkey
  • Avocado
  • Lettuce and tomato
  • Mustard or mayo

Instructions:

  1. Spread mustard or mayo on two slices of bread.
  2. Layer sliced turkey, avocado slices, lettuce, and tomato on one bread slice.
  3. Top with the second slice of bread and cut in half.
