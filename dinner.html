Satisfying Dinner Recipes

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A rich and creamy pasta dish that's easy to make and always satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta or bacon until crisp; add garlic.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan, then combine with hot pasta off the heat.
  4. Add pancetta or bacon and adjust seasoning.
  5. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese.
